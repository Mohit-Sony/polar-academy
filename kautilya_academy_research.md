## 6. Course Offerings
Kautilya offers structured, multi-tier programs for different grade levels:
- **PRE-FOUNDATION (Class VIII, IX, X):** Focuses on solidifying fundamental concepts to ensure a seamless transition to competitive studies in later years.
- **FOUNDATION (Class XI & XII):** Comprehensive preparation for JEE post-class 10. It covers the standard School/Board Exam syllabus along with remedial classes for strengthening basics.
- **TARGET (Class XII Passed/Droppers):** Intensive post-class 12th JEE preparation (including BITSAT), starting from scratch with a strict, time-bound approach.
